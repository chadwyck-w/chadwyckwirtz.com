Nokia N1 Tablet

The Nokia N1 is an Android-powered tablet developed by Nokia. Unveiled on 18 November 2014, it is Nokia's first mobile device since the sale of its original mobile phone business to Microsoft earlier in the year
I was invited to join a team within Nokia design focused on rapid prototyping and and technical design support. This was an exciting opportunity for me that I believed would more closely align with my interests and skill set.
My first project was to help create a number of prototypes for exploring how the Nokia Z-Launcher could be best adapted to a tablet form factor. This product would be known as the Nokia N1 Tablet, it was developed in partnership with an ODM and its form factor very closely modeled the iPad Mini. The device was high quality, but could be sold at a lower price point than the comparable iPad.
Our final prototype expanded the interaction of Z-Launcher to include a more conventional home screen and widgets while staying in line with the original Z-Launcher principles. The device was released with aplomb in China.
