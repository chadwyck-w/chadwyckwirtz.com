LG webOS

webOS, also known as LG webOS and previously known as Open webOS, HP webOS and Palm webOS, is a Linux kernel-based multitasking operating system for smart devices such as smart TVs and it has been used as a mobile operating system
The webOS redesign of the LG smart TV resulted in a radical simplification of the user experience. The state of the art LG Smart TV had good industrial design and best in class panel but the user experience was severely challenged. At odds with the physical quality of the TV, the software was complex, poorly executed and lackluster in performance.
The redesign took the staple webOS concept of application cards to an entirely new level of dynamism. Even today it continues to stand out from its peers staid designs. When first I saw the nascent design I immediately volunteered to create an interactive prototype.
I created a fully interactive demo based on the design. A motion tracking tracking remote control similar to the Magic Remote used by LG was used as the input. Working with motion designer I was able to replicate the design in high fidelity and with live content.
I demonstrated the prototype to a number of LG executives including the visiting president of our organization.
