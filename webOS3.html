I participated in a number of design and engineering exercises focused on improving and expanding the webOS experience. This is just a small sample of my projects from that time.
Vertical Tabs in a browser
I created a prototype web browser to test if moving browser tabs from the top of the chrome to the side could improve the user experience. The idea was that moveing the tabs to the side would increase the amount of vertical screen space for content without detriment to the horizontal component of the content
Users noticed the improvement in vertical browser space, but found the vertical tabs difficult to distinguish from one another. Another noticable deficit with vertical tabs was the amount of space a large number of vertical tabs would take up on screen.
