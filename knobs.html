Knobs

A polyphonic synth featured in the app store.
I was interested in learning about audio synthesis by way of iOS development. I discovered an open source monophonic audio synthesis library and integrated it into the design that I created for an expressive audio synth for the iPhone and iPad.
This app was released to the app store in 2010 and to my surprise it showed up in the New & Noteworthy section of the app store music category.
Being featured in the app store led brief period of high sales. When sales completely tapered off I removed the app from the store temporarily and released it as a free app with ads as an experiment. There was a fair number of downloads in the first few days which were quickly followed up with poor reviews reflecting the addition of the distracting ads. I deleted the app from the store shortly after as I considered the experiment complete.
